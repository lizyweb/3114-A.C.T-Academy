A.C.T ACADEMY

The A.C.T. Academy is a registered organisation and initiative that aims to improve and promote skills among research scholars and teaching professionals in a wide range of fields. The Academy serves as a link between academia and the industry, with the goal of equipping students, teachers, and professionals working in educational field with the knowledge and abilities they need to succeed in fields that are constantly evolving.
