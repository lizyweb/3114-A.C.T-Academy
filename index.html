About Us

Achieving Excellence Together

The A.C.T. Academy is a registered organisation and initiative that aims to improve and promote skills among research scholars and teaching professionals in a wide range of fields. The Academy serves as a link between academia and the industry, with the goal of equipping students, teachers, and professionals working in educational field with the knowledge and abilities they need to succeed in fields that are constantly evolving. Since October 2022, we have been conducting skill-based workshops and faculty development programmes for faculty members and scholars. Those who serve as our resource persons are professionals and professors from various industries and reputed universities. The academy has successfully completed a total of 34 online programmes and 2 hybrid mode programmes thus far. Programmes are meticulously organised and executed with utmost attention to detail, including the collection of feedback, distribution of session recordings, and proper issuance of certificates.

Our Academic Services

Faculty Development Programmes

Our faculty development programmes are tailored to upgrade teaching methodologies and enhance the overall skill set of educators. We focus on improving classroom engagement, research output, and leadership abilities to drive academic excellence.

Training Programmes/Workshops

Engage in dynamic, hands-on training and workshops that provide practical knowledge and skills. These sessions are designed to bridge the gap between theoretical understanding and real-world applications, ensuring holistic learning experiences for participants.

Seminars

Participate in insightful seminars led by industry experts and scholars. These sessions provide a platform for academic discussions, debates, and networking opportunities, enabling participants to stay updated with the latest trends in their respective fields.

Conferences/Symposiums

Attend our academic conferences and symposiums that focus on cutting-edge research and scholarly work. These events bring together experts and innovators to exchange knowledge, discuss emerging trends, and foster collaboration across disciplines.

Faculty Enrichment Programmes

Our faculty enrichment programmes are aimed at enhancing the academic and research capabilities of educators. These programmes ensure continuous professional development, fostering innovation, and excellence in both teaching and scholarly activities.

Students Development Programmes

Our student development programmes focus on cultivating the academic, personal, and professional growth of students. These programmes are designed to enhance their critical thinking, communication, and problem-solving skills, ensuring all-round development.

Publications

Our publications offer in-depth insights into emerging academic trends, research methodologies, and pedagogical advancements. We aim to foster intellectual growth and encourage continuous learning through our comprehensive range of scholarly works.

A.C.T.A Classic Publisher

A.C.T.A Classic Publisher is dedicated to advancing academic scholarship through high-quality publishing services. We provide a platform for researchers, scholars, and professionals to disseminate their work effectively, contributing to the global body of knowledge.

Book Chapter Publication

Our Book Chapter Publication service enables authors to present their research and insights in curated volumes. We accept a diverse range of topics and disciplines, ensuring your work reaches the right audience. Each chapter undergoes a rigorous peer review process, ensuring high standards of quality and relevance. Our expert editing team will refine your manuscript, enhancing clarity and coherence, while our professional formatting services ensure a polished final product. By publishing with us, you join a community of thought leaders contributing to ongoing academic discourse.

Book Publishing Process

Our streamlined Book Publishing Process is designed to guide authors smoothly from manuscript submission to final publication. Each stage is carefully managed to uphold the highest quality and integrity. It begins with your submission, followed by a comprehensive initial review and rigorous peer evaluation. After receiving feedback, authors can make necessary revisions to strengthen their work. Our professional editing team will then enhance the manuscript’s clarity and presentation. Finally, we facilitate the publication and distribution of your book across multiple platforms, maximizing visibility and accessibility.
